✦ Our Story ✦

Born from a Passion for Scent

Lumière began in a small kitchen in 2019, where our founder Priya poured her first candle for a friend's birthday gift. The response was overwhelming — everyone wanted one.

Today, we blend over 50 unique fragrances, sourced from sustainable farms around the world. Every candle is hand-poured in small batches to ensure quality and consistency.

We believe that scent is one of the most powerful triggers of memory and emotion. Our mission is to help you fill your space with moments worth remembering.
